Steps

  1. Put all bagel dough ingredients in a breadmachine. Mix and knead the dough for 20 minutes.
  2. Let the dough rise for 1.5–2 hours.
  3. Divide the dough into 8–10 piece. Shape each piece into a small ball. Cover with food wrap. Let the balls sit for 20 minutes.
  4. Shape each ball into the bagel shape. Cover with food wrap.
  5. While the bagel dough is resting, boil a pot of water. Once the water is boiled, put in baking soda and brown sugar.
  6. Once the baking soda and brown sugar are dissolved in the water. Turn the heat to low. Put in the bagel dough one by one. Boil each side of the bagel dough for 1 minutes. Then, take the dough out. Put on any topping you'd like.
  7. Preheat the over to 375°F. Put a piece of parchment paper on the baking tray. Place bagel on the parchment paper. Bake for 18 minutes.
  8. Take the bagels out of the over. Let it cool and store them in paper bags or freeze them.
