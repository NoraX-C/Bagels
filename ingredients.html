Ingredients

Please measure the amount of each ingredient.

For the bagel dough

  • 800g bread flour
  • 400g water
  • 6g yeast
  • 30g honey
  • 10g salt

For boiling the bagel

  • 2tbs baking soda
  • 10g brown sugar

Optional: for bagel topping

  • Seasame seeds
